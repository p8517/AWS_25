Q1.What is Cloud?
Cloud is the on_demand availability of compurter system resources,
especially data storage and computing power.without direct active
management by the user.The term is generally used to describe data
centers available to many users over the internet.

1.Private Cloud
2.Public Cloud
3.Hybird Cloud
